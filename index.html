Everything you need to know about the LED Lightbox Kit

The LED Lightbox Kit features an adjustable color temperature range from 3200K to 5600K, allowing you to match any lighting environment. At 3200K, you get warm, tungsten-like tones perfect for indoor portraits and intimate settings. At 5600K, you achieve daylight-balanced lighting ideal for outdoor photography and general studio work. The smooth transition between these temperatures gives you complete creative control over the mood and atmosphere of your photographs. This versatility makes it suitable for virtually any photography scenario, from warm candlelit portraits to cool, crisp product shots.

The built-in lithium-ion battery provides up to 8 hours of continuous operation at full brightness on a single charge. If you're running at 50% brightness, you can extend the runtime to approximately 12-14 hours, making it ideal for full-day shoots. The battery charges completely in just 2-3 hours using the included USB-C fast charger. For extended sessions or backup power, we recommend purchasing our optional extended battery pack, which doubles your runtime. The intelligent battery management system provides real-time status indicators on the control panel, so you always know how much power you have remaining.
