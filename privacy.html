Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our Site. They contain information about your browsing activity and preferences. Cookies help us remember your preferences, improve your browsing experience, and provide personalized content and advertising.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies for various purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the Site to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. These cookies cannot be disabled.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our Site, such as which pages you visit and any errors you encounter. This data helps us improve Site performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable us to remember your preferences and provide enhanced features, such as remembering your login information or language preferences.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver targeted advertisements based on your interests and browsing history. They may be set by us or by third-party advertising partners.

Third-Party Tracking Technologies

Our Site may contain tracking technologies from third-party service providers, including:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site. Google may set cookies and use similar tracking technologies to collect and analyze usage data.
  • Social Media Pixels: We may use tracking pixels from social media platforms to measure advertising effectiveness and retarget visitors with relevant content.
  • Advertising Partners: Third-party advertisers may set cookies to track your activity across websites and deliver personalized advertisements.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect the functionality of our Site and your ability to access certain features. For more information about managing cookies, visit the help section of your browser or consult allaboutcookies.org.
